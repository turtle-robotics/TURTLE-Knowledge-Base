On-Campus Fabrication Resources
----------------------------

.. list-table::
   :widths: 10 20 15 55
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Abbreviation
     - Location
     - Resources
     - Costs/Limits
     - Notes
   * - TURTLE Lab
     - [TURTLE Robotics](https://www.turtlerobotics.org/)
     - HEB 023
     - 3D printing, project storage, basic hand tools
     - Free to TURTLE members, open to requests.
     - Primary space for work, limited space and resources.
   * - FEDC
     - [Fisher Engineering Design Center](https://fedc.engr.tamu.edu/)
     - ZACH 1st Floor
     - Mills, lathes, waterjet, laser cutting, 3D printing
     - Membership training + hourly costs
     - Great place to learn tools; effectively capstone-only access.
   * - RPS
     - [MEEN Rapid Prototyping Studio](https://wiki.rapidprototypingstudio.com/)
     - JCAIN 421
     - 22.04 (Jammy)
     - Stable, widely used; supported through May 2027.
   * - LTS
     - Noetic Ninjemys (ROS 1)
     - 20.04 (Focal)
     - Legacy ROS 1; maintenance ends May 2025.
   * - STS
     - Iron Irwini (ROS 2)
     - 22.04 (Jammy)
     - Short-term support; bridge path toward Jazzy.
